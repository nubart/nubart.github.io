If I buy several basic packages, will I be licensed for more than two guides?

Yes. Each order of a basic package includes a permanent license for two guides. If you buy two packages you will get a license for 4 guides. But you will not be able to change or manage those guides: they are fixed.

Do I have to buy the 500 cards of the basic package even if I only have an event with a few people?

Yes, the Basic package is our minimum order. Please note that the cards do not expire: you can keep the extra cards for future events.

Can I buy the basic package cards and print them to my liking?

No. If you want cards with your brand, colors or logo, you will have to choose the Medium or Professional package.

Do the cards have an expiration date?

No. Regardless of the package you choose, you only have to buy the cards once and keep them until you need them. When you run out, you can order a replacement. If you have chosen the Medium or Professional package, you will have to renew the guides' license annually.
