Multi-Method Triggered Commentary + AI Translation for Sightseeing Vehicles

Nubart provides multilingual audio tours for river cruises, tour buses, and sightseeing vehicles—without on-board speakers, PA systems, or app downloads.

Passengers scan a QR code to access professional audio commentary in their preferred language, delivered directly to their smartphones.

Choose your solution:

  • For automated tours: Pre-recorded professional commentary with multiple triggering methods for reliable playback anywhere—from GPS-based automation to manual control. We'll recommend the best approach for your specific route during your free custom demo.
  • For human-guided tours: AI-powered real-time translation of your guide's voice into multiple languages. Guides speak naturally—passengers hear seamless translation instantly.

Flexible business models: Sell as premium add-on, include with ticket, or offer as complimentary service.

Nubart TRANSLATE: Real-Time Multilingual Translation for Live Guides

Make your live guided tours accessible to everyone—regardless of language.


Simultaneous Translation for Live Guides

With Nubart TRANSLATE, your guide's spoken commentary is translated in real time using advanced AI technology into multiple languages. Passengers simply scan a generic QR code and instantly receive the guide's words in their preferred language—delivered as both audio and text directly on their smartphones.

  • No app downloads or special devices required
  • Wide language coverage for international guests
  • Instant access via a simple QR scan
  • Translations delivered as audio and/or text (accessibility!)
  • Seamless integration with your human guide
  • Requires solid internet connection

Ensure every guest enjoys your tour—without language barriers.

Nubart MOTION: Multi-Method Triggered Multilingual Audio Tours

Offer fully automated, multilingual audio tours with flexible triggering: GPS, manual remote control, automatic remote control, and timed sequential activation.


Automated, Multilingual Audio Tours

With Nubart MOTION, passengers enjoy high-quality, prerecorded commentary in their preferred language, triggered by your choice of four activation methods: GPS location-based, manual remote control, automatic remote control via master device, or timed sequential triggers. Content is preloaded during internet-covered areas, ensuring seamless offline playback even in challenging connectivity environments.

  • Four triggering methods: GPS, manual remote, automatic remote, and timed sequential
  • Combinable triggering approaches for optimal reliability
  • Offline mode with content preloading for uninterrupted tours
  • No PA systems, loudspeakers, or passenger app downloads required
  • Passengers select their language for personalized experiences

Perfect for self-guided tours, hop-on/hop-off operations, and operators seeking reliable, scalable solutions that work in any environment.

Common questions from tour operators

Since launching Nubart MOTION, we've helped river cruise companies, sightseeing bus operators, and boat tour providers transition from traditional PA systems and hardware-based audio guides to our QR-based solution. Here are the questions operators most frequently ask us:

Shortly after filling out the form, you'll receive an email requesting information for your personalized demo based on which solution interests you:

For Nubart TRANSLATE (live guide translation):
You'll receive a guide login and a QR code to test AI-powered translation free for 30 cumulative minutes. You can split this test time across several days to experience the system in real tour conditions.

For Nubart MOTION (automated commentary):
We'll ask you for:

  • A list of 5-6 main points of interest along your route (names and brief descriptions)
  • Your route map (PDF, image, or Google Maps link)
  • Tour duration, required languages, and vehicle type

Within a few days, you'll receive a custom QR code with 5-6 points of interest activated using the triggering methods we consider most suitable for your specific case. You'll test this under real conditions—on your vehicle during an actual route.

Of course, you can also request a demo for both options.

Whoever you prefer! You have complete flexibility:

Use your existing content: If you already have audio commentary from a previous system, we'll gladly incorporate it into Nubart MOTION.

Produce it yourself: If you want to create new content in-house, no problem—we'll integrate whatever you provide.

Let us produce it for you: Our professional content production team includes experienced scriptwriters, translators, and voice-over artists with strict quality control. We offer two options:

  • Premium production: Fully human-created, professionally voiced content
  • AI-assisted production: AI-generated content reviewed and enhanced by our human team for quality assurance at a lower cost

Timeline depends on your chosen solution and content readiness:

Nubart TRANSLATE (live guide translation): You can implement our AI-powered translation system immediately.

Nubart MOTION with existing content: Approximately 2 weeks to configure your automated triggered audio guide.

Nubart MOTION with new content production: 3-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of your route and number of languages required.

No problem! We offer a "Custom Geolocation for Nubart MOTION" package that includes up to 2 route adaptations per year at no additional cost.

If you need more frequent updates, additional adaptations are available for €500 each.

Internet requirements vary by solution:

Nubart TRANSLATE (live guide translation): Yes, your vehicle must have reliable internet access throughout the entire route—either Wi-Fi or mobile data—for real-time AI translation to function.

Nubart MOTION (automated triggered tours): Minimal internet required. Passengers only need good connectivity at the beginning when they scan the QR code, as the entire system preloads onto their smartphones. During the route, very low bandwidth is sufficient for the system to receive triggering signals from the remote control.

No internet at all? We can configure a time-triggered system that works completely offline—no internet connection required during the tour.

Our free custom demo will help us determine which triggering method is most suitable for your specific route and identify any potential connectivity issues.
