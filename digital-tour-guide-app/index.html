Digital Tour Guide System - Nubart Live

  1. Home
  2. Digital Tour Guide System - Nubart Live


Replace traditional tour guide tools with smartphones


Are you guiding a group in a noisy environment? Or in an environment that requires silence or very quiet speech?

Have you been put off by the price of conventional tour headsets ?

Nubart® Live is what you need. It's an Internet-based solution that works easily on the participant's smartphones, without requiring the download of an app! And it's very easy to use, even for non-techies.

A cardboard printed card with a unique code connects the guide to the tour participants. The guide's smartphone acts as a sender and the visitors' smartphones as receivers.

It's as simple as that. Now your tour guide can comfortably speak without having to shout.

As simple as it gets

Why an audio tour guide device if everybody has a smartphone?

Easy to use

Both visitors and the guide scan the unique QR-code on the cards. That's it! No registration or app download required. (Only the tour guide has to register).

Affordable

Our Basic package, with 500 cards, costs less than renting a tour guide system and will cover you for many trips.

Anonymous

Your visitors do not need to provide any personal information to use Nubart Live. It's completely anonymous!

Ask the guide

Your guests can ask the guide a question at any time without interrupting him: The guide can access the list of accumulated questions at any time.

See how it works

Add an audio guide

In our Medium and Professional packages, you can also include an extra module with a multimedia prerecorded audio guide.

Easy to carry

A suitcase with a tour guide whisper system is heavy to carry and attractive to thieves. Not our cards!

Hygienic

A conventional tour guide headset must be sanitized after each use. Not our cards!

Eco-friendly

Devices generate harmful electronic waste. Our cards are printed on FSC cardboard with CO2 compensation.

Use Cases

Factory tours

Guide your visitors around your plant in a very cost-effective way:
Combined with a unidirectional smartphone microphone, Nubart Live is ideal for tours in noisy environments.

Simultaneous translation

Nubart Live can save you the costly rental of portable translation equipment:
Each interpreter registers and acts as a guide. Simply distribute the cards to your audience grouped by language.

City tours

Nubart Live is a simple and affordable wireless communication system for tourist tours through the city:
Forget the traffic noise and don't worry if someone wanders too far.

Accesibility

Nubart Live helps you be more inclusive:
The use of headphones on a smartphone during a tour provides a much better experience for people with hearing impairments (no interference from background noise, volume adjustment).

Questions for the guide? No problem

Most standard tour guide systems (devices) don't allow guests to ask questions.

Others have a push-to-talk button that allows the guest to broadcast a question to the entire group. However, shy participants may be reluctant to interrupt the guide. And the more daring can take over the tour and ask endless questions, making the experience worse for everyone.

Nubart Live offers a non-invasive solution where questions are collected on the guide's smartphone and the guide can answer them at the appropriate time.
