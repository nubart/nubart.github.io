Factory Tours Without Expensive Radio Guides

Factory tours are essential for showcasing your plant and educating visitors, but noisy environments can make communication challenging. A reliable, clear, and interactive tour experience is crucial—especially for new international employees who may not speak the local language.

While traditional radio guide systems have been the standard, they are costly to purchase or rent and require ongoing maintenance. These devices can also limit your ability to deliver content in multiple languages.

Nubart LIVE offers a modern, cost-effective alternative for factory tours. Simply provide our cards, an internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data), and let visitors use their own smartphones—no app download required. The system works entirely in a browser, with instant connection between guide and audience via QR codes on the Nubart cards.

For an even more inclusive experience, our optional AI-driven translation add-on delivers live translation in over 33 languages—perfect for training employees from abroad who may not yet speak the local language. This feature supports clear, effective communication and makes onboarding new international staff easier than ever.



Optional AI-Powered Simultaneous Translation Add-on: Perfect for Multilingual Factory Training


Improve your factory tours and training sessions with Nubart LIVE’s optional AI-powered simultaneous translation add-on. Eensure every new employee—regardless of their native language—understands your instructions and safety protocols.

The process is just as simple as the standard Nubart LIVE experience. Participants scan the QR code, and then choose their preferred language:

Train and onboard international teams efficiently—without the need for multiple interpreters or complex setups. With Nubart LIVE, one guide can communicate clearly and effectively, ensuring every participant receives the information they need in their preferred language.

Ask the guide? No problem

In a noisy environment, visitors need to be able to ask questions without shouting. Most traditional radio guides do not allow questions to be asked. Or, if they do, they occur in real time and interrupt the guide. Many visitors don't dare to do so and remain silent.

Nubart Live offers a non-invasive solution where questions collect on the guide's smartphone so that the guide can answer them at the appropriate time.
