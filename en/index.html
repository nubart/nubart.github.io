The audio guide card

We enable museums - small and large - to offer audio guides without dedicated devices nor developing expensive apps. An attractive card with a unique code is the visitor’s gateway to rich content. The audio tracks are streamed directly to the visitor’s own smartphone. Our audio guides are inexpensive and profitable for the museum.

We do all the work! Included in our prices are evaluation, translation and voice-overs of the content.
