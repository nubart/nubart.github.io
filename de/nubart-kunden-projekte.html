The Thinking Machine: Ramon Llull and the 'ars combinatoria'
(CCCB - Barcelona. 14 July - 11 Dec. 2016)

Description

This temporary exhibition was hold at the Centre de Cultura Contemporània (CCCB) in Barcelona. We produced the audioguide in Spanish, Catalan, English, German and French. The Spanish and Catalan version were enriched with comments by the curator, Amador Vega.

1,000 m2 of desire. Architecture and sexuality
(CCCB - Barcelona. 25 Oct. 2016 - 19 March 2017)

Description

This temporary exhibition took place at the Centre de Cultura Contemporània (CCCB) in Barcelona. We produced the audioguide in Spanish, Catalan, English and French. To the conventional audio tracks we added recordings by different specialist involved in the exhibition, like curators, specialist, artists...

3D. Printing the World
(Espacio Fundación Telefónica - Madrid. June 2016 - Oct. 2017)

Description

This interesting temporary exhibition around three-dimensional printing technology and its impact on several disciplines took place at the Espacio Fundación Telefónica in Madrid. We produced the audioguide in Spanish and English.

Link to the exhibition

History of Telecommunications
(Espacio Fundación Telefónica - permanent exhibition)

Description

This interesting permanent exhibition about the history of telecommunications can be seen at the Espacio Fundación Telefónica in Madrid. We produced the audioguide in Spanish and English. For the first time we produced audiotracks with sound effects, like the noise of a rotary or the ambience of an old switchboard room.

Link to the exhibition

Thermalia Museum
(Permanent exhibition)

Description

This permanent exhibition in the lovely Thermalia-museum in Caldes de Montbui (Barcelona) explains two topics: the history of their thermal baths and the friendship between Picasso and Manolo Hugué. The museum provided their own voice overs in Spanish, Catalan, French and English.

Audio Route
Nexo Turismo y Cultura

Description

This is our first Audio Route. Its digital content (audio, pictures and maps) will guide you through one of the most beatiful landscapes in Spain: the natural monuments of El Maestrazgo in Teruel. Since the data coverage in El Maestrazgo is very bad, we developed a new offline mode, no app needed! Our customer wrote the script and Nubart produced maps, translations and voice overs in Spanish, French and English.

Website Nexo Turismo y Cultura

Permanent Exhibition
Museu Faller de València

Description

Have you ever been at the Fallas in Valencia? In that case you will know about the Ninots, the carefully crafted figures that end up in the fire every year. All except the best one. The Museu Faller in Valencia keeps all the saved figures, and by doing so it also guards the recent history of the city. In Nubart we produced the audio-guide in Valencian, Spanish, Italian, French and English.

Link to Las Fallas' Website

Dinosfera (Coll de Nargó, Lleida)

Description
