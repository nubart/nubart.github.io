Museu Faller de València

Description

Have you ever been at the Fallas in Valencia? In that case you will know about the Ninots, the carefully crafted figures that end up in the fire every year. All except the best one. The Museu Faller in Valencia keeps all the saved figures, and by doing so it also guards the recent history of the city. In Nubart we produced the audio-guide in Valencian, Spanish, Italian, French and English.
