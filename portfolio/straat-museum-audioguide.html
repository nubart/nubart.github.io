The STRAAT's audio tour card

STRAAT is a graffitti and street art museum in Amsterdam. STRAATS resides in a 8000 m2 former warehouse. It currently displays more than 150 artworks by 130+ artists that were created on-site. STRAAT was voted Best Museum the Netherlands 2021 at the Tiqets Remarkable Venue Awards.

Nubart produced the content in English and Dutch based on a script provided by the customer. Our voice-over artists did a great work conveying the youthful and streetwise accent most appropriate for such a museum.
