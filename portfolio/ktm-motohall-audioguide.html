KTM Motohall's audio guide card

The spectacular building conceived by the architects of Atelier Brückner in 2019 and located in Mattighofen, Austria, houses the historic motorcycle collection of the popular motorcycle manufacturer KTM. The interactive exhibition on three levels allows visitors to know everything about the bikes, the adventures of the motorsport heroes and the history and technology of the KTM brand.

Nubart's audio guide card delivers Motohall's permanent exhibitions in German and English, including content for kids. Several images sliders with subtitles displays the best pieces in the collection and an interactive map helps with the orientation.
