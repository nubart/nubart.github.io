Cave of Campanet's audio guide card

The Caves of Campanet are located in the north of the island, in an unspoilt valley in the mount of Sant Miquel. They have an area of 3,200 m2 and was discovered by chance in 1945. Several endemic species have been found in the cave. Magnificient stalagmites and stalactites sculpted by water throughout millions of years can be admired by visitors.

Nubart produced translations and voice overs in English, Spanish, Catalan and German based on a script provided by the customer. The audio guide is in offline mode due to data coverage problems related to the geology of the cave.
