British Cemetery's audio guide card

This romantic cemetery, located in the neighborhood of Carabanchel in Madrid, was founded in 1853. It houses the tombs of foreigners, mostly British Protestants who formerly could not be buried in Catholic cemeteries. The moving stories of some of the people buried here are recorded in Nubart's audio guide. The cemetery can be visited on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays from 10:30 am to 1:00 pm.

The audio guide has been produced by Nubart in English and Spanish based on a script provided by the client. It includes an interactive map so that visitors can easily find the location of each tomb.
