Dornier's audio guide card

The Dornier Museum in Friedrichshafen documents the pioneering work of aircraft designer Claude Dornier and the former Dornier-Werke company.

Nubart has produced the audio guide in German, English and French based on a script provided by the client. The audio tracks have been recorded by several voice artists and the integration of original sounds. The audio guide includes numerous digital features, such as a second level of information, "before and after" images, three-dimensional elements and buttons for file sharing or voice messages.
