By signing below, the signatory confirms they are authorized to act on behalf of the data controller and accept all conditions set out herein.

Customers whose workflows require systematic transcript generation, archival, or post-event documentation should consider dedicated transcription platforms designed specifically for persistent content retention and document production.

1. BACKGROUND

Nubart TRANSLATE is a real-time AI simultaneous interpretation service. Its standard data processing activities are designed so that event content is processed transiently, in accordance with the retention option selected by the Customer at the time of purchase.

Post-event transcript generation is not a standard deliverable of Nubart TRANSLATE and is not included in the scope of the Customer's existing DPA. Where a Customer explicitly requests a transcript, Nubart may accommodate this on a strictly exceptional, case-by-case basis, subject to execution of this Authorization.

This Authorization constitutes a documented instruction from the Controller to the Processor pursuant to Article 28(3)(a) GDPR, authorizing the specific additional processing activities described herein.

3. CUSTOMER ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

By signing this Authorization, the authorized signatory confirms, on behalf of the Customer as data controller, each of the following:

  1. Explicit request and legal basis. The Customer explicitly requests that Nubart generate a post-event transcript for the event identified in Section 2. The Customer confirms that this request constitutes a lawful documented instruction under Article 28(3)(a) GDPR and that the Customer has identified a valid legal basis under Article 6 GDPR for this additional processing.
  2. Extended retention. The Customer acknowledges that transcript generation requires Nubart to temporarily retain and process event data beyond the transient processing model of the standard service configuration. The Customer authorizes Nubart to retain the relevant event data for as long as is necessary to generate and deliver the transcript, and instructs Nubart to delete all event data used in this process within 30 calendar days of the transcript delivery date.
  3. Additional processing activities. The Customer acknowledges that transcript generation involves processing activities not covered by the standard DPA, including deduplication, reformatting, and AI-assisted post-processing of raw event output. The Customer authorizes these additional activities solely for the purpose of generating the transcript requested herein.
  4. Additional processing tools. The Customer acknowledges that transcript generation may require the use of additional processing tools or sub-processors beyond those used for the standard Nubart TRANSLATE service configuration. Where applicable, Nubart will ensure that such processing is subject to appropriate contractual and technical safeguards consistent with applicable data protection law.
  5. Quality and nature of output. The Customer acknowledges that Nubart TRANSLATE is a live simultaneous interpretation system. Its output reflects the nature of real-time AI processing and does not constitute professional transcription. The transcript is delivered as generated, without certified editorial review. It does not constitute official minutes or a certified record of the event.
  6. Test session content. The Customer acknowledges that raw event data may contain content from technical test sessions conducted before or after the event. Nubart will make reasonable efforts to exclude identifiable test content but cannot guarantee complete separation.
  7. No liability. The Customer agrees that Nubart bears no liability for errors, omissions, or inaccuracies in the transcript, nor for any consequences arising from its use for any downstream purpose.
  8. Authorization to act on behalf of the data controller. The signatory confirms that they are duly authorized to give instructions to Nubart on behalf of the Customer as data controller, and that this Authorization has been reviewed and approved at the appropriate level within the Customer's organization.
  9. No precedent. The Customer acknowledges that Nubart's acceptance of this Authorization does not create any obligation or expectation that transcript generation will be provided for future events, and does not modify the standard scope of the Nubart TRANSLATE service or DPA for any other purpose.
