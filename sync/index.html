How do visitors hear a silent screen in their own language?




Hard to believe? Try our demo!

Your video is playing silently on a public screen — in a museum, a shop window, an event, or a city square.

A passerby scans a QR code. Instantly, they hear the soundtrack of your video — in their own language, on their own phone, perfectly timed to what's on screen.

This is Nubart SYNC: a web-based service that adds multilingual sound to any silent video screen. It streams each video's audio to your visitors' own phones and keeps it timed to what plays on the display — no app, no headphones, no special hardware.

No app to install. No WiFi broadcast. No beacons. No receivers. Just a browser and a connection.

Our patented technology streams audio to any number of smartphones at once, staying tightly synced to the video with no perceptible drift — even over long loops or on a screen the size of a building.

Use Nubart SYNC for digital signage, video walls, outdoor projections, museum installations, and accessibility — anywhere a silent screen should speak, clearly and in any language.

Give a Voice to Your Silent Screens

Why choose Nubart SYNC over headphones or broadcast hardware?

How can you play audio for a public display without hardware or an app?


Public spaces are full of muted screens — digital signage, ads in shop windows, safety instructions at airports, documentaries in waiting rooms. But sound is often forbidden due to noise regulations or social etiquette.

Nubart SYNC solves this with a clean, user-friendly solution: stream audio directly to smartphones via QR code. Visitors scan, select their language, and listen — all in perfect sync with the video.

No special hardware. No Bluetooth, beacons, or apps. Just the video, a QR code, and our cloud-based synchronization technology.

You can embed the QR code on the screen itself, or place it nearby. It works equally well in both indoor and outdoor environments — even with large screens, mosaics, or projection mappings.

How Is Audio Paired with Video Today?

And why Nubart SYNC changes everything.

The Old Way

Most current solutions require complex hardware: dedicated media players, RF/IR systems, RFID triggers, or iBeacons.

Some offer Wi-Fi broadcast — but only through captive portals, unreliable connections, or custom apps.

It’s expensive, high-maintenance, and rarely user-friendly.

Nubart SYNC is not an app, not an FM or infrared assistive-listening system, and not a Wi-Fi broadcast. It streams from the cloud to any phone's browser.

Nubart SYNC delivers multilingual, lip-synced audio straight to browsers — no app, no login, no friction.

It works instantly on any smartphone with internet access. Fully scalable. Shockingly simple.

How Nubart SYNC Works

From silent screens to synchronized multilingual audio

Nubart SYNC works by allowing visitors to scan a QR code with their smartphones. They watch a muted video on-site while Nubart's cloud system streams the synchronized audio in their preferred language directly to their device, with no apps or hardware required.

What video players work with Nubart SYNC?

Compatible video player options:

Any screen or player with internet connection

If your device can open a modern browser and has an Internet connection, it should be compatible with Nubart SYNC.

BrightSign

Compatible with BrightSign video players with Internet connection, such as HD224. Compatible with BrightAuthor digital signage software.

Micro-mapping or other AV-solutions

As long as your device has Internet connection, you can integrate our API in your custom playback system. Please contact us.

Audio playback systems compatible with Nubart SYNC

Compatible audio player options:

Any smartphone

Nubart SYNC soundtracks open in a browser. So any smartphone with a browser and the ability to scan a QR code is compatible. No app to download required!
