The self guided campus tour visitors keep

We enable universities and colleges to offer their campus-visitors an on-site guiding system without any devices or apps.

An attractive, non-transferable branded card with a unique code allows visitors to access prerecorded tracks or to listen to a human guide directly on their smartphone. No extra hardware required!

Visitors get information about the campus in their native language, directly on their smartphones.

Or your visitors listen to your volunteer guide by also keeping social distance.

You will get great insights through visitor usage data and feedback on the prerecorded audio guides.

And you will able to monitor your human guides remotely, so that you can improve their training.

The troubles of guided campus tours



A campus tour is often the blind date of the admissions process.

Not every volunteer student guide has the skills to market the school effectively or to answer sensitive questions.

Most of your guides will not have the language-knowledge to cover the needs of the multicultural populations you would like to attract.

And there is not always a guide available.

Our Nubart campus-guiding-system allows you to solve all these problems:

Nubart offers a prerecorded audio and multimedia guide with geolocation for self guided visitors or when a guide is not available.

Nubart also offers a monitored live guiding system so that your visitors can keep social distancing when listening to the from their own smartphone.

Nubart as a campus self guided tour



Nubart is the audio guide your campus visitors keep.

Our audio guide cards are extremely easy to use, even for will help you to create a memorable guidance that will have a positive influence on enrollment decisions.

With Nubart you have a guide that never gets sick, that’s available 24/7, that says exactly what you want to be said and that speaks in any native language of your visitors!

No more struggle to find a volunteer when a family visits the campus during the summer, weekends or other off hours, or when the season is so busy that you don’t have enough guides for all.

And you can have even your most famous and busy professors sending greetings personally to all your visitors.

Enhance your campus visits



When a human guided tour is not possible, some colleges hand out a brochure with a map as a self guided tour. With Nubart you can do much better!

  • Integrate digital maps with a route, so that no visitor gets lost.
  • Include as many audio tracks as you want: we can record them professionally in any language based on a script. You can also send us audio-messages from your celebrities or alumni!
  • Highlighting automatically the track that corresponds to the nearest POI with our geolocation.
  • Do you have a museum or art gallery at the campus? One of the modules on the card could be its audio guide!
  • Customized list of interests, allowing the visitor select only information about the majors he/she is considering to take!
  • Videos, on-site PDFs, image sliders, downloadable files, donation button…
  • And to make it fun: highlight selfie opportunities at your venue with our take a selfie here-functionality!

Use Nubart as a complement to your guided tours



Nubart does not intend to substitute your human guided tours: but if the group is joined by a foreign family, your guide can give them a Nubart card. In this way they can still can follow, but listen to the prerecorded explanations in their native language. The rest of the group can listen directly to the guide by using our digital live-guide feature!

Since Nubart is an audio guide that visitors can keep and reuse, the card is not only a nice branded souvenir:

It will help visitors to consolidate the memory of what they have seen by revisiting the campus later on.

Don’t worry, the cards are non transferable: your valuable content will stay with the visitors that came physically to your venue.

Get to know your college visitors

You already have tools to analyse who is visiting your website. Now Nubart offers you the tool you needed to analyse your on-site visitors! Find out their country of origin, their native language, how long they stay at the venue, which tracks do they listen…

Each of our guides includes a customized feedback form, so that your visitors can tell you what they liked the most about the tour. You can even receive feedback in real time via email!
