Use Cases

Museums

Nubart GUIDE is an accessible and easy-to-use solution for visitors to museums, castles, exhibition centers, monuments, and historical sites.

On-site Campus Tours

With Nubart GUIDE, you can provide a complete interactive on-site navigation solution for students and families who wish to take self-guided tours of your university campus.

Factory and plant tours

With Nubart LIVE, you can train your new employees or guide your visitors around your industrial facilities: our smartphone system works in noisy environments and offers real-time translation into 33 languages.

Boat cruises and bus tours

Nubart MOTION offers an audio guide with real-time translation or automatic activation for boat trips, tourist buses, historic trains, or any other vehicle offering tourist routes.

Zoos and Aquariums

The combination of Nubart GUIDE and Nubart GAME offers a monetizable interactive digital navigation and gamification solution for zoos, aquariums, and safaris.

Observation decks, rooftops and viewpoints

Enhance rooftop attractions with interactive 360° views for an elevated guest experience.
