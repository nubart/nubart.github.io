Network with the right people !


Are your event attendees tired of spammy contacts? Or of wasting valuable time talking to the wrong people? Do they need to access quickly and on the spot only the people that really add value?

Our branded card with a unique code allows to browse and swipe quickly hundreds of attendees, prioritized by interests and needs in common. Directly from the smartphone, without having to download an app!

No more classifying hundreds of businesscards after an event.

Since our Nubart-cards are non-transferable, they can be sold and provide ROI.

How does it work

Get the card

Each attendee receives a cutomized card with an individualized code upon arrival at the event's venue.

Scan the QR or enter a code

The attendee scans the QR code.
If QR-codes are unfamiliar to her, she can also enter a numeric code on the event's website.

Set up your profile

You can use your profile on Linkedin or make a new one in a few quick steps.

Select your interests

The list can be customized and adapted for each event.
For trade-shows we have two lists: "What do I offer" and "What do I need"

Swipe the attendees

The order of the attendees will be prioritized by the interests you have in common.
Quickly swipe all the attendees right or left.

Network!

If the attendee also swiped you to the right, you will get a match. Then you will be able to access her contact data to start a conversation right away.
