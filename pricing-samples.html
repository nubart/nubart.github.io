Audio guides

All our audio guide cards are personalized. Our minimum fee is a one-time payment of €/$ 5,000 (no ongoing costs!). It includes 3,000 printed cards (which you can give away or sell), the design of the card and the digital setup of the audioguide by our team, incl. future changes.

Group Guiding System (Nubart Live)

Our minimum fee for non-personalized cards with license for up to 2 guides is a one-time payment of €/$ 400 plus shipping.

Personalized cards with license for up to 10 guides are available from €/$ 4,000.
