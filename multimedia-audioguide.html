Multimedia elements and services that can be integrated into a Nubart audioguide

The flexibility of the Nubart multimedia audio guides allows for the integration of audio along with text, images, videos, files, links and many more. All those elements can be integrated in any desired order.

Please scroll through out list of multimedia elements. Imagine how complete your audioguide could be with all of them!

Modular System


Your venue may have several exhibitions at the same time and should display more than one audio guide.

To make this possible, we offer a modular system, so that the same Nubart card can include several guides. You can also incorporate our tour guide system as a separate module.

Within each module you can incorporate any of the elements listed below:

Collapsable more-information-button


The owner of a Nubart card can consult the audio guide at home at any time. It is worthwhile to take advantage of this and provide a second level of insight into each of your exhibits without distracting from the tour during the on-site visit. Our collapsible "more information" button makes this possible.

You can place any kind of multimedia information behind this button: more audios, videos, images, PDFs...

Tagged audio guide


Let your users choose what really interests them!

Our tagging system allows you to limit the displayed content to the user's wishes.

This is very useful at trade shows, but also for very rich audio guides with a lot of content.

If they change their mind, users can select a new list of interests at any time. The number of tags is unlimited.

Audio tracks


At Nubart we use an in-house audio-player that works well with any browser.

A blue frame indicates which track is currently being played.
Audio tracks are generally recognized by image, number and/or name of the exhibit or point of interest.

Images


You can choose small thumbnails or larger images associated with the point of interest.

You can send your images in any size, since our CMS will adapt them automatically.

Image slider


You can put up to 15 images in this image slider! The slider is controlled by the user. By using the image subtitles, you can show a chronological development.

Before / after images


Has your venue changed over time? Do you want to show the progress of an archaeological excavation? Do you want to show a painting before and after a restoration?

With our function before / after, of great educational value, the visitor can slide a central bar with his finger to see the change.

Panoramic 360° images


Those images are immersive and allow the visitor to "move inside" a 360° panoramic representation of your venue.

On many smartphones the image will move automatically, generating a nice wow-effect. On others the user can move the image with the touchscreen.

Videos


You can send us exclusive video-files to be loaded in our private server. Those would be only available in the audio guide; they are not accessible publicly and can not be shared.

Video and audio live streaming


Are you organising a conference and you would like to broadcast the keynote live?

That's no problem with our live-streaming-feature. We can stream several speeches at the same time.

We can also offer live streaming for audio with practically no latency.

Info button


Sometimes a little additional information can be very helpful. That's why we've created our information button. There you can place, for example, opening hours or contact details. Phone numbers or email addresses are clickable.

The pop-up window disappears automatically by touching the screen.

Transcriptions for the deaf


An icon gives you access to the full transcript of the audio track in the corresponding language.

A scrollable window will open. By closing the window you return to the audio guide.

Maps


Maps are a must for city-tours. They are also useful to direct the visitor towards landmarks or buildings outside the exhibition.

We integrate interactive Google maps with visitor's location.

Geo-scrolling


Our geolocated audio tracks light up in red and scroll automatically to the top of your mobile screen as you approach the exhibit or location. In Android, this process is enhanced by a slight vibration of the mobile phone.

In this way, your visitor always knows which track she is supposed to listen to next. They do not play automatically to enable a non-intrusive functionality.

On site PDF reader


The audio guide can include one or more PDFs to be read directly on site: general information about the museum, legal attributions or papers by the curator or artist about a specific exhibition.

Our online PDF reader also allows downloading.

This feature even allows your audio guide to replace the catalog. Since the digital content can remain available even after the exhibit, cards for past exhibitions can be sold in the museum shop.

File upload button


Your visitors can share files with your museum. For example, they can upload historical documents or family photographs to enrich your collection. Or upload photos to participate in a contest your museum has launched.

You will receive a notification each time someone uploads a file. The files go through an antivirus and are deposited in your client area for your review.

Voice message button


Your visitors can spontaneously record a voice message for your museum. For example, they can give their testimony about a historical event.

You will receive a notification each time someone has left a voice message. The recordings go through an antivirus and are deposited in your client area for your review.

Donation button


Our audioguides can help our customers' revenue through the resale of the non-transferable cards, but also through a donation button integrated on its digital content.

Just create a donation button from your own Paypal account or another similar service and send us the generated code.

Subscription forms


Would you like to ask visitors to subscribe to your newsletter or to include any other form requesting personal data in your guide?

At Nubart we follow a strict policy regarding personal information, but we can embed your own subscription form as long as it is GDPR-compliant. This way your prospects' private data stays only with you!

Of course, we can also embed other materials you may have: 3D images of your venue, interactive screens...
